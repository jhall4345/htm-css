Vicious Cycles - About Us

Welcome to The Vicious Cycles Family!

Are you thinking of buying a new bike or getting your current bike serviced? There are so many options available for brands, styles, and upgrades. We know it can be overwhelming and that is what we are here to help simplify for you. There is only one option for your best local bike shop and pillar of the cycling community and thats us, Viciuos Cyclces! We have been located in Raleigh and doing our best to take exceptional care of our customers for the past 11 year and we are looking forward to many more years to come. Vicious Cycles was started as a family business and remains that way today, only our family has grown. Alot! We'd like to invite you to become a part of our community. We are not just here to sell bikes, but to connect with people and to encourage that connection to grow within your life and within the community we create together. Join us for upcoming events, weekly meetups at the shop, and local rides!
